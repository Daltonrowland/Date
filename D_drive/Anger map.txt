2. The Anger Circuit (A → B → C)

A. The Spark Layer (0–2 seconds)

This is the instant chemical surge.

Signals:
	•	Heat rising
	•	Jaw clench
	•	Chest pressure
	•	Short breath
	•	“What the hell?” thought
	•	Flash of energy

This stage is pure biology.
Not conscious. Not personal.

This is where 90% of conflict is born.

⸻

B. The Filter Layer (2–12 seconds)

This is where YOUR emotional architecture decides what to do next.

The system checks:
	•	“Am I unsafe?”
	•	“Was I disrespected?”
	•	“Was I ignored?”
	•	“Did someone break the code?”
	•	“Does this threaten my identity or order?”

If yes → escalates.
If no → diffuses.

This is where couples either:
	•	fight
	•	freeze
	•	shut down
	•	or collaborate

⸻

C. The Output Layer (12 seconds → minutes)

This is the “behavior stage.”

Output types:
	1.	Fire: yelling, snapping, venting
	2.	Ice: shutting down, withdrawal, silence
	3.	Armor: sarcasm, cold tone, defensiveness
	4.	Redirect: “Nah I’m good…” but clearly not good
	5.	Leadership: calm clarity, boundary-setting
	6.	Repair: “Let’s just solve the problem”

Each archetype has a default output.
Yours?
Armor → Leadership.
You rarely “explode.”
You go straight to protecting order.
