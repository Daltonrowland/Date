⭐ 2. THE STRUCTURE — 30 Days, 3 Themes, 10 Days Each

DAYS 1–10: The Past that Formed You

Soft, non-threatening questions like:
	•	“What did love feel like in your early life?”
	•	“A moment that shaped your expectations.”
	•	“Something you learned too early.”
	•	“A memory you carry but never talk about.”

This fills Past Patterns, Childhood Imprints, and Early Love Style in the Vault.

Depth Score rises gently here — fastest growth.

⸻

DAYS 11–20: The Present you’re navigating

These are about behavior, not trauma:
	•	“What your heart does when it feels unsafe.”
	•	“A habit you wish people understood about you.”
	•	“How you show affection without saying it.”
	•	“A moment recently when you felt small, or proud.”

This fills Emotional Reflexes, Triggers, Anchors, and Attachment Tendencies.

Depth Score sharpens the archetype & shadow accuracy.

⸻

DAYS 21–30: The Future you’re growing toward

These are spiritual and forward-facing:
	•	“What you hope love feels like next time.”
	•	“A version of you you’re slowly becoming.”
	•	“What you need to feel safe with someone.”
	•	“What you’re ready to release.”

This fills Spirit Indicators, Healing Readiness, Intentions, Values.

Depth Score hits maximum clarity here.
