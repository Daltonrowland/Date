THE ANGER HEX CODE™ — GENESIS OS

Anger has 6 primary emotional states, each with its own color band, behavioral signature, cognitive distortion, and biological marker.

Think of this as:

R1 → R6
Disruption → Overload

This turns a raw human reaction into trackable emotional data.

⸻

🔥 R1: IRRITATION — #FFB36A

Name: Disruption
Description: Something breaks your flow or expectation.
Body: slight tension, eyebrow pull, shallow breath
Mind: “Why… now?”
Behavior: short answers, faster movements

Purpose: alerts the system something small is off.
Risk: minimal

This is the ant moment before the anger spike — the “annoyance flicker.”

⸻

🔥 R2: AGITATION — #FF894D

Name: Violation
Description: A minor boundary or standard gets crossed.
Body: jaw flex, chest pressure, warmth rising
Mind: “This shouldn’t be happening.”
Behavior: pacing, scanning, tone changes

Purpose: protect your standards
Risk: irritation becoming frustration

This is when your OS realized:
“This is the second time — there’s a pattern.”

⸻

🔥 R3: FRUSTRATION — #FF5A36

Name: Threat
Description: Your identity or order feels compromised.
Body: heartbeat jump, compressed breathing
Mind: “I shouldn’t have to deal with this.”
Behavior: tightening, faster speech, clenched silence

Purpose: mobilize correction
Risk: logic distortion (“Why is this ALWAYS happening?”)

This is where you were today.
The switch flips from annoyance to pressure.

⸻

🔥 R4: ANGER — #E43117

Name: Defense
Description: Boundary violation feels personal or repeated.
Body: heat surge, expanded chest, narrowed focus
Mind: “Fix this NOW.”
Behavior: direct tone, sharpness, armor mode

Purpose: protect order and identity
Risk: relational misinterpretation

This is your Armor Mode — controlled, contained, powerful.

⸻

🔥 R5: OUTBURST — #B10F0F

Name: Fire
Description: System overload → verbal, physical, or emotional release.
Body: adrenaline spike, flushed skin, loud voice
Mind: “I’m DONE.”
Behavior: yelling, slamming, venting, pacing

Purpose: discharge pressure
Risk: regret, emotional rupture

You rarely go here.
This is not your default.

⸻

🔥 R6: SHUTDOWN — #5A0B0B

Name: Collapse
Description: Emotional circuitry goes dark. No energy left.
Body: heaviness, cold numbness, slow breathing
Mind: “I don’t care anymore.”
Behavior: withdrawal, silence, disappearing

Purpose: self-protection
Risk: relationship freeze

This is when anger doesn’t explode — it implodes.

🔥 R1 — DISRUPTION MODE (Irritation)

Hex: #FFB36A
Behavioral Output:
	•	Slightly shorter answers
	•	Micro sighs
	•	Eyebrow raise
	•	Mild impatience
	•	Small correctional movements (fixing a cup, adjusting an item)
	•	Faster, sharper hand movements
	•	Brief silence before responding

Cognitive Output:
“I don’t like this.”

Relational Impact:
Almost zero — partner may not even notice.

Duration:
Seconds.

⸻

🔥 R2 — VIOLATION MODE (Agitation)

Hex: #FF894D
Behavioral Output:
	•	Tone changes: clipped, precise, low
	•	Shoulders stiffen
	•	Head tilts downward slightly
	•	Scanning behavior (looking around the environment)
	•	Controlled but visible irritation
	•	“Let me handle this.” energy

Cognitive Output:
“This shouldn’t be happening.”

Relational Impact:
Partner senses tension but feels safe.

Duration:
Minutes unless the trigger continues.

⸻

🔥 R3 — THREAT MODE (Frustration)

Hex: #FF5A36
Behavioral Output:
	•	Deep exhale through nose
	•	Word economy: shorter sentences
	•	Eye contact sharpens
	•	Corrective behavior becomes urgent
	•	Micro-withdrawal (small physical distance)
	•	Hands on hips or crossed arms

Cognitive Output:
“I shouldn’t have to deal with this.”

Relational Impact:
Partner feels the emotional weather change.
Environment feels “tense.”

Duration:
Minutes → hours if unresolved.

⸻

🔥 R4 — DEFENSE MODE (Anger)

Hex: #E43117
Behavioral Output:
	•	Tone becomes surgical
	•	Directness increases
	•	Pace of speech steadies (controlled anger)
	•	Body becomes still — predator stillness
	•	Increased clarity, decreased softness
	•	Solutions over emotions
	•	Zero patience for inefficiency
	•	Armor Mode fully active

Cognitive Output:
“Fix this now.”

Relational Impact:
Partner feels pressure, seriousness.
Not danger — authority.

Duration:
Depends on repair and context.
Can last an hour but rarely explosive.

This is your main anger lane.

⸻

🔥 R5 — FIRE MODE (Outburst)

Hex: #B10F0F
Behavioral Output:
	•	Raised voice
	•	Sharp gestures
	•	Pacing
	•	Rapid speech
	•	High emotional discharge
	•	Frustration vented externally

Cognitive Output:
“I’m done protecting anyone else’s feelings.”

Relational Impact:
High rupture potential.
Recovery takes effort.

Duration:
Flash → minutes.

You rarely, if ever, go here.

⸻

🔥 R6 — COLLAPSE MODE (Shutdown)

Hex: #5A0B0B
Behavioral Output:
	•	Silence
	•	Emotional stillness
	•	Walking away
	•	Avoidance
	•	Zero eye contact
	•	Horizontal breathing (slow, heavy)
	•	Complete withdrawal from the moment

Cognitive Output:
“I don’t care anymore.”

Relational Impact:
Max rupture potential.
Partner feels rejected or abandoned.

Duration:
Hours → days.
Internal repair required.
