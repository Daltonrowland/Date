## Full Description (4000 characters)

Relationship Scores is not another swiping app. It is the world's first compatibility platform powered by Genesis OS — a patented emotional intelligence engine that processes psychometric, behavioral, astrological, and spiritual data to generate real compatibility scores on a 350 to 850 scale.

HOW IT WORKS

Take a 60-question assessment that goes deeper than any dating quiz you have ever seen. The questions explore your attachment style, communication patterns, conflict resolution approach, emotional regulation, values alignment, love language preferences, and shadow behaviors. Every answer maps to one of 10 emotional archetypes and 6 shadow types, creating a complete emotional blueprint unique to you.

Your answers are processed through 48,205 pre-computed answer pair quanta — the same scoring engine used in relationship research — to calculate precise compatibility scores with every other user on the platform.

YOUR COMPATIBILITY SCORE (350-850)

Your score is not a guess. It is a deterministic calculation across 13 weighted dimensions including archetype fit, communication health, emotional regulation, love-relational alignment, readiness, shadow compatibility, integrity, cluster patterns, pacing, polarity chemistry, pursuit rhythm, and cosmic overlay from zodiac and numerology data.

Six compatibility tiers tell you exactly where you stand:
• Soul-Aligned (750-850) — Rare deep alignment across all dimensions
• Strong Potential (650-749) — Strong emotional fit with real capacity
• Healthy & Growing (550-649) — Solid foundation with room to grow
• Magnetic but Risky (450-549) — High chemistry but stability needs work
• Possible but Unstable (400-449) — Potential exists but patterns need settling
• Red Flag Zone (350-399) — Significant misalignment requiring growth work

10 ARCHETYPES

Discover which of the 10 Genesis OS archetypes defines your relational pattern: Analyzer, Fixer, Icebox, Performer, Phantom Seeker, Quiet Exit, Regulated Grown-Up, Romantic Idealist, Survivor, or Translator. Each archetype comes with strengths, growth edges, and specific pairing insights with other archetypes.

THE KNOCK SYSTEM

Scores below 550 require a Knock before messaging — protecting both people's emotional bandwidth. This is not a paywall. It is an intentional friction point that ensures conversations only happen when both people are open to the connection despite lower initial compatibility.

SANCTUARY MODE

Your private emotional space. Use Sanctuary for personal reflections, emotional state tracking, goal setting, and when you are in a relationship, shared couple tools including goals, milestones, love language alignment, and private notes.

RS CODE IDENTITY

Every user receives a unique 6-character RS Code — your private identity token across the platform. Share it with friends to connect directly. Look up anyone by their RS Code.

POST-MATCH CALIBRATION

After exchanging messages, the Genesis OS calibration system checks in with you. Your honest feedback about conversation quality, energy match, and behavioral signals adjusts your dynamic compatibility score in real time. Feedback is always private and never shown to the other person.
